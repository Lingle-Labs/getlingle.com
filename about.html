About

Lingle is an AI-powered language learning app that helps you learn through writing. Created by two passionate students who believe that the best way to learn a language is through natural expression and personalized feedback.

Ari Yamashita (Class of 2027)

Visit Instagram

Ari is a student at Eastside Preparatory School interested in learning languages and coding. He learned coding when he was very young but had never developed in Swift before learning it for this project. Ari has always wanted to build an app, he was just waiting for the right idea. Now, he is very excited about the potential of Lingle to become a groundbreaking AI language learning app. When he is not coding, Ari is also an actor, cellist, and Ultimate Frisbee player.

Nikhil Mahesh (Class of 2027)
