Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Lingle ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Lingle iOS app processes, how it is used, and what choices you have.

1) Scope

This policy applies to the Lingle iOS app.

2) Information We Process

A. Information you provide in the app

Depending on how you use Lingle, the app may process:

  • Profile details you enter (for example, first name and last name)
  • Optional profile photo you choose from your device
  • Learning content you type, such as:
    • journal entries
    • translation answers
    • in-app responses for quizzes and practice

B. Learning and progress information

The app stores learning-related state, including:

  • language preferences
  • level and XP/progress indicators
  • challenge completion, best times, streak history, and tutorial status
  • saved vocabulary and saved articles

C. Device-level permissions (optional)

  • Photos access (only if you choose to upload a profile photo)
  • Notifications (only if you allow reminders)

D. Network data

When Lingle calls remote endpoints, your device necessarily shares standard network metadata (such as IP address and user agent) with those services.

3) How Information Is Used

We use information processed by the app to:

  • personalize your in-app experience
  • run core learning features (journal feedback, translation validation, news-based exercises, placement/benchmarking)
  • save your progress and content for later use
  • send local reminder notifications when enabled

4) Where Data Is Stored

  • On-device storage: Lingle stores profile and learning state locally on your device (for example, app preferences and local app database content).
  • Remote processing: Some features send the minimum required input to backend APIs to generate results (for example, journal analysis, translation validation, benchmark, and news-question generation).

5) Third-Party Content and Services

Lingle fetches learning content through remote services, including news/article content. When articles are fetched from external websites, those sites may independently collect technical data according to their own privacy practices.

6) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We share data only as needed to provide app functionality (for example, with backend services used to process learning requests and return results).

7) Data Retention

  • Data stored on your device remains until you delete it in-app, uninstall the app, or clear/reset device/app data.
  • Data sent to backend endpoints may be retained for security, reliability, and service-improvement operations, consistent with applicable law.

8) Children

Lingle is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

9) Your Choices

You can:

  • choose whether to provide profile details or a photo
  • disable notification permissions in iOS Settings at any time
  • stop using networked features by avoiding actions that submit content to remote APIs
  • uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data from your device

10) Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures intended to protect information processed by the app. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

11) International Users

Because Lingle relies on internet-based services, data may be processed in countries other than your own, subject to applicable legal requirements.

12) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a revised "Last updated" date.

13) Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact:

  • Email: admin@getlingle.com
