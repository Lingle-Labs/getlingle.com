What is Lingle?

Lingle is an AI-powered language learning platform for learners of all levels.

Daily Journal

Practice your skills by answering fun journal prompts. Enhance your writing with grammar checks and a built-in dictionary. AI grades your journal and provides detailed feedback.

Translation Games

Race around the track and beat your highscore. Translate AI-generated phrases to accelerate.

Lingle by Ari Yamashita and Nikhil Mahesh.
